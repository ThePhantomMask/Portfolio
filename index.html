Scaryland: Forbidden Park

Scaryland: Forbidden Park is an Adventure Horror with storydriven focused set in an abandoned theme park cursed by a mysterious presence. The gameplay is inspired by Silent Hill and Resident Evil and Siren games, and with the music segments it becomes a rhythm game inspired by Gotarro Man and Taiko no Tatsujin. The graphical style is an homage to the games of the 32/64-bit era (N64 and PS1) and is also inspired by children '92s cartoons and anime, but in a horror setting.

In production

  • Role: Creator, Gameplay Programmer
  • Technologies: C#,C++
  • Engine: Flax Engine
