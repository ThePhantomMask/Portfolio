My Work

Scaryland: Forbidden Park

Scaryland: Forbidden Park is an Adventure Horror with storydriven focused set in an abandoned theme park cursed by a mysterious presence. The gameplay is inspired by Silent Hill and Resident Evil and Siren games, and with the music segments it becomes a rhythm game inspired by Gotarro Man and Taiko no Tatsujin. The graphical style is an homage to the games of the 32/64-bit era (N64 and PS1) and is also inspired by children '92s cartoons and anime, but in a horror setting. I made it with the Flax Engine Game Engine and in development.

  • C#

Connection Lost

Connection Lost is a Sci-Fi First Person Puzzle Game in which the player is a robot who cannot move in an abandoned factory where he must repair the electrical system with his PipeLauncher.

What I developed:

  • Logic for the PipeLauncher functions such as firing, colours if the beam interascends with the wall.
  • The UI element of when you pick up a pipe to shoot it
  • The area that eliminates pipes when they fall when the conveyor channel ends
  • Role: "Gameplay Programmer"
  • Technologies: "C++/Blueprint"
  • Engine: "Unreal Engine 5"
